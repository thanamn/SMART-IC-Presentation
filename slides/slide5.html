Section II: SMART-IC View

Overall Framework

SMART-IC builds a closed loop from production traces to factory decisions.

1

Trace Collection

Sensors and testing phases generate heterogeneous production traces across the fab.

2

AI-Powered Engine

Deep learning extracts anomalies, forecasts, and synthetic data from those traces.

3

Meta-MES Action

Scheduling, maintenance, and plant reconfiguration react to the extracted knowledge.
