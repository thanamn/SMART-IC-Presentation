Section III-B: Anomaly Detection

From Error to Decision

Turning reconstruction error into a binary anomaly alarm.

Decision Rule

The anomaly score is the reconstruction error. If it exceeds a threshold, the trace is flagged as faulty.

IF (RE > Threshold) THEN Anomaly

The chosen threshold depends on how much false-alarm risk the fab can tolerate.

Why ROC and AUC?

ROC and AUC compare models across all possible thresholds, so they measure discriminative power without committing to one operating point.

  • Threshold-free view: useful before deployment choices are fixed.
  • Best results: AE and VAE both reached AUC = 0.90.
