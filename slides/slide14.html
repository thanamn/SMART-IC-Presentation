Part IV: Summary & Conclusion

Integrated Framework and Open Challenges

SMART-IC combines IoT, Artificial Intelligence, and data analytics in a one-of-a-kind loop from fab data to factory management.

IoT Data

Production traces and testing information describe the current condition of the fab.

→

AI Knowledge

Deep learning extracts alerts, forecasts, and other useful knowledge from those traces.

→

Meta-MES Action

Scheduling, maintenance, and production reconfiguration react to that knowledge.

Why DATE Feedback Matters

SMART-IC is ambitious and still presents important challenges. The paper explicitly values interaction with the DATE audience to identify those challenges early and tackle them more effectively.
