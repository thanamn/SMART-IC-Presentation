Part IV: Summary & Conclusion

Why SMART-IC Matters to DATE

Section IV frames SMART-IC as a strategic contribution to the digital transformation of semiconductor manufacturing.

Strategic Target Sector

SMART-IC focuses on semiconductor manufacturing, where smarter decisions can improve both industrial performance and broader digital competitiveness.

  • Efficiency: better coordination of fab operations and resource usage.
  • Reliability: more robust production and maintenance decisions.
  • Sustainability: lower waste, fewer unnecessary actions, and improved lifecycle management.

Benefit to the Community

The paper explicitly connects SMART-IC to the Design, Automation and Test in Europe community and to the wider field of digital technologies.

Its expected impact reaches both DATE researchers and practitioners working on design, automation, test, and intelligent industrial systems.
