Part II: SMART-IC Framework

The "Meta" Upgrade: Beyond Traditional MES

Transforming passive tracking into autonomous supervision.

Traditional MES

A passive recording system. It tracks what happened and where wafers are, but requires manual intervention for every decision.

  • Static Scheduling
  • Manual Maintenance Dispatch
  • Zero awareness of AI alerts

SMART-IC Meta-MES

An active "Layer 2" controller that intercepts AI insights to execute factory-floor actions autonomously.

Reconfig
Reroutes wafers if a machine is degrading.
Sched
Dynamic production priority based on yield.

Proactive Mitigation

Instead of stopping the line AFTER a fault (reactive), the Meta-MES applies **Mitigation Strategies** (re-allocating resources) BEFORE the fault affects a single lot of wafers.
