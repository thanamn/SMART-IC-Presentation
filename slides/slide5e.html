Section II-A: AI-Powered Engine

Three Core AI Tasks

The paper expands the AI engine beyond fault diagnosis into prediction and data generation.

Anomaly Detection

Learn regular behavior from non-faulty processes, then detect discrepancies offline or in real time.

Forecasting

Predict the future evolution of sensed variables to estimate upcoming risk and degradation.

Synthetic Generation

Create realistic traces to address missing data, strengthen training, and support digital-twin use cases.

These three outputs later feed Meta-MES decisions.
