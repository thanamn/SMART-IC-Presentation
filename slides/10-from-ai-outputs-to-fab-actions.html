Section II: SMART-IC View

From AI Outputs to Fab Actions

AI Engine Outputs

  • Anomaly detection: offline on full traces or online on sensor streams.
  • Production forecasting: future process evolution and degradation risk.
  • Synthetic generation: new traces for augmentation and digital-twin support.

Meta-MES Reactions

  • Smart maintenance: reduce unexpected downtime and maintenance costs.
  • Line reconfiguration: reallocate production when risk or quality changes.
  • Advanced scheduling: combine AI insights with system and process models.

Why This Matters

Testing outputs and trace-based metrics can also annotate final products and improve the next round of model training.
