The Throughput Pressure

With equipment this expensive, the primary goal is maximizing WPH (Wafers Per Hour). Any maintenance-induced downtime is a direct hit to the ROI of the entire facility.

SMART-IC aims to protect this investment by ensuring testers run at peak efficiency with zero unscheduled breaks.
