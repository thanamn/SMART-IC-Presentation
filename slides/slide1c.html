Section I: Introduction

Testing Is Essential and Expensive

Effective chip testing protects product quality, but the required equipment is itself a major industrial investment.

Why Testing Matters

  • Quality assurance: detect issues before products reach the market.
  • Process visibility: reveal abnormal manufacturing behavior.
  • Parallel execution: automated equipment tests many chips efficiently.
