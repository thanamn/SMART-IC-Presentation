Section I: Introduction

Testing Is Essential and Expensive

Effective chip testing protects product quality, but the required equipment is itself a major industrial investment.

Why Testing Matters

Testing protects product quality, reveals abnormal manufacturing behavior, and lets automated equipment evaluate many chips efficiently in parallel. Because ATE and handlers are expensive assets, it also creates strong pressure for smarter monitoring and better equipment use.
