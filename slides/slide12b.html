Part III: Preliminary Study

Production Data Forecasting

Predicting The Future

Instead of just flagging a fault that has already occurred, the engine uses historic dependencies to forecast the future trajectory of sensor variables.

Key Architectures

  • LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory): Excels at capturing temporal dependencies in time-series data.
  • Auto-regressive Models: Uses past states to iteratively predict the next timestamp.

Business Value

Accurately estimates Remaining Useful Life (RUL) of equipment.

This allows maintenance to be scheduled precisely before failure occurs (Proactive) rather than after a breakdown halts the $7B fab (Reactive).
