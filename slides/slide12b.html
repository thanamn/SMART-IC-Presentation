Section III-C: Production Data Forecasting

Predicting Future Production Data

Forecast Task

The same ML backbones can be trained to predict a future signal X(t1) from the current one X(t0) and, when useful, from past context.

  • Short horizon: predict the next sample or next window.
  • Wider horizon: predict larger portions of the trace or even future lots.

Why It Matters

Forecasting extends the engine from present-state monitoring to future-state anticipation.

By applying anomaly detection on forecast data, SMART-IC can warn about degrading behavior before it becomes a visible fault and support earlier production or maintenance decisions.
