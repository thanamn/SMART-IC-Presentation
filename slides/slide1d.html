What is a Pseudo-fail?

Occurs when a perfectly functional wafer is flagged as "defective" due to miscalibrated testing equipment or unnecessary cleaning cycles.

  • Triggered by univariate SPC failing to interpret complex sensor noise.
  • Wastes hundreds of thousands in sellable silicon.

The SMART-IC Fix

By using multivariate AI Engine alerts, the system distinguishes between a true process fault and common sensor drift.
