Section I: Introduction

The Gap SMART-IC Tries to Close

The paper positions SMART-IC as a comprehensive framework, not an isolated optimization tool.

Limits in Prior Efforts

  • Many works target only specific semiconductor processes.
  • Others focus on scheduling or maintenance without using rich production data effectively.

SMART-IC Position

SMART-IC aims to close the loop with a complete framework where AI and advanced production management cooperate on the same manufacturing scenario.

The ambition is to connect sensing, intelligence, scheduling, and maintenance in one system.
