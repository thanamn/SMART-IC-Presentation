Part III: Preliminary Study

Synthetic Data Generation

The Data Imbalance Problem

In high-yield fabs, "faulty" data is extremely rare. Models need examples of failures to learn, but producing real failures ruins expensive wafers.

Generative Solutions

Using GANs (Generative Adversarial Networks) and Diffusion Models trained on noise, conditioned to synthesize highly realistic traces of faulty wafers without destroying real silicon.
