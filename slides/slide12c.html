Section III-D: ML-Based Synthetic Data Generation

Generating New Production Traces

Generation Task

A generative model starts from random input, such as Gaussian noise, and outputs a new trace that mimics the training distribution.

Conditioned Output

GANs and Diffusion Models can be conditioned with labels, so the generated trace belongs to a chosen class such as normal or faulty.
