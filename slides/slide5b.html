Part II: SMART-IC Framework

The Two Pillars of SMART-IC

1. The AI Engine

The predictive "brain" processing multidimensional sensor traces. It performs three main tasks:

  • Anomaly Detection: Identifying faulty processes in real-time.
  • Data Forecasting: Predicting future sensor states & failure times.
  • Synthetic Generation: Augmenting data & building Digital Twins.

2. The Meta-MES

What is an MES? A Manufacturing Execution System controls the factory floor, tracking and documenting the transformation of raw materials to finished goods.

The "Meta" Upgrade: Transforms a static MES into a dynamic supervisor that autonomously schedules maintenance, reroutes wafers, and reconfigures production based directly on AI alerts.
