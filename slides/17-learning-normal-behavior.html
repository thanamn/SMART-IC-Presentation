Section III-B: Anomaly Detection

Learning Normal Behavior

Normal-Only Training

The model is trained on non-faulty traces so it can learn what a normal production behavior looks like.

Deviation = Anomaly

At run time, traces that deviate too much from the learned normality are flagged as anomalous events.

Tested Models

  • Autoencoder (AE): classic reconstruction-based model.
  • Variational Autoencoder (VAE): probabilistic latent representation.
  • Vector-Quantized VAE (VQ-VAE): discrete latent encoding.
  • OmniAnomaly: recurrent generative modeling for time series.
