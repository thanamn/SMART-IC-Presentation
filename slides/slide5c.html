Section III-A: Scenario

Preliminary Study Scenario

The paper uses a public semiconductor dataset to illustrate SMART-IC on a realistic early-stage case study.

Production Setting

  • Wafers and lots: wafers move through common production steps and are grouped into lots.
  • Sensor monitoring: traces capture gas flow, temperature, voltage, and related process variables.
  • Ground truth: post-production tests label each wafer as faulty or non-faulty.

Dataset Snapshot

  • 54 lots collected in sequence.
  • 25 wafers per lot in the preliminary study.
  • Two process steps: SPUT with 32 sensors and RTP with 24 sensors.
  • 176 s traces sampled every 1 second.
Illustrative wafer process flow to anchor the lot-and-step terminology used in the case study.
