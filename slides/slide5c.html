Part III: Preliminary Study

Case Study Scenario

Leveraging real-world sensor data from semiconductor frontend production.

The Dataset

Building on top of public and industrial data representing a typical wafer fab scenario:

  • Processes: Deposition (SPUT) & Rapid Thermal (RTP).
  • Sensor Traces: Gas flow, temperature, and voltage.
  • Timestamp: 1-second resolution.

Multi-dimensional time series per wafer.

Scale of Study

The preliminary analysis covers 54 successive lots, each consisting of 25 wafers. Each wafer is annotated with binary ground-truth labels (Faulty vs. Normal) obtained from post-production tests.
