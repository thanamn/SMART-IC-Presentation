Section II-B: Meta-MES

Dynamic Scheduling and Feedback Loop

The paper frames Meta-MES as both an optimizer and a data amplifier for the AI engine.

AI Events

Alerts, risk metrics, and quality indicators arrive from the AI-powered engine.

→

Digital Twin

→

Machinery data, trace metrics, and testing outputs are historicized by Meta-MES, compared across production stages, and reused to create new training data for the AI engine.
