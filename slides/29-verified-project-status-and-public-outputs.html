Verified Project Status and Public Outputs

Public sources confirm the funded project window and show a clear research trail around SMART-IC, but not a public final product release.

Officially Verified Record

  • Project code: PRIN 2022 SMART-IC, 2022T7YSHJ.
  • Funded window: September 28, 2023 to September 27, 2025.
  • Coordinator: Politecnico di Torino, with Padova and Verona research units.
  • Public budget: 285,899 euros listed on the UNIPD project sheet.
  • As of March 25, 2026: the public-facing legacy is mainly papers and enabling methods; We could not verify a public final report, software release, or fab-scale deployment summary.
