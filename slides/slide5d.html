Part II: SMART-IC Framework

Univariate vs. Multivariate Analysis

The leap from traditional SPC to Deep Learning.

Traditional Industry (SPC)

  • Univariate Statistics: Monitors one variable at a time (e.g., Gas Flow only).
  • Blind to Correlation: Misses faults hidden in the interaction BETWEEN gas, heat, and voltage.
  • Manual De-correlation: "Proving" these models in real-world messy data is nearly impossible.

The SMART-IC Advantage

  • Multivariate Time Series: AI "sees" all 50+ sensors simultaneously in a single coherent space.
  • Latent Patterns: Captures intricate dependencies and complex "fingerprints" of failure.
  • Implicit Learning: No need for de-correlation; the neural network learns to ignore noise automatically.

"Modern ML captures intricate patterns within complex multivariate datasets that traditional statistics simply cannot see."
