Part III: Preliminary Study

Prognostics: Forecasting & Synthesis

Moving beyond detection into future anticipation.

Production Forecasting

Training models to predict future signals $X(t_1)$ from current/past data $X(t_0), X(t_{-1})$:

  • Multi-Scale: Predict single timestamps or entire future lots.
  • Anticipation: Identifies degrading trends *before* they cross the anomaly threshold.

Conditioned Data Synthesis

Using GANs & Diffusion to solve the Data Scarcity problem:

  • Labels as Inputs: Models are conditioned with "Normal" or "Faulty" labels to generate specific distributions.
  • Yield Boost: Synthetic data allows "digital training" of new tools without wasting silicon.

This prognostic strategy allows Meta-MES to implement targeted maintenance policies weeks in advance, optimizing the entire fab’s lifecycle.
