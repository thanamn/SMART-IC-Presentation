Part III: Preliminary Study

Moving to Generative Models

Why Not Classification?

Traditional classifiers require balanced datasets (50/50 normal vs failed). They completely fail to generalize on 99.9% skewed fab data.

The Generative Approach

Models are trained only on normal production data to learn "healthy" distributions. Anything deviating is flagged as an anomaly.

Selected Architectures

  • Autoencoders (AE): Bottleneck compression.
  • Variational AE (VAE): Probabilistic latent spaces.
  • DAGMM: Deep Autoencoding Gaussian Mixture Model for complex distributions.
