Part IV: Summary & Conclusion

Core Topics for the DATE Conference

SMART-IC sits at the intersection of several technology areas that are central to the DATE community.

IoT

Connected sensing and monitoring provide the real-time industrial data needed for fab awareness and decision support.

Artificial Intelligence

Deep learning methods turn raw manufacturing data into actionable knowledge for prediction, diagnosis, and adaptation.

Data Analytics

Analytical pipelines connect observations, models, and operational decisions across the production lifecycle.

The paper highlights this convergence of IoT, Artificial Intelligence, and data analytics as a key reason SMART-IC is relevant to DATE.
