Reflection

What Is Still Missing

The limitations are understandable for an initial-stage project, but they still matter when interpreting the contribution.

No Full Fab Validation

The paper does not yet show deployment on a real semiconductor production line with plant-level outcomes.

Uneven Evidence

Anomaly detection has first results, but forecasting, synthetic generation, and Meta-MES optimization are still mostly future-facing.

No KPI Closure Yet

The paper motivates efficiency, reliability, and sustainability, but it does not yet quantify those gains with operational KPIs.

So this should be read as an early framework and feasibility paper, not as the final proof of deployment.
