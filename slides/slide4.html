Section I: Introduction

Why AI and ML Fit Semiconductor Manufacturing

Data-Rich Manufacturing

Semiconductor manufacturing and testing generate large amounts of complex data, which makes them well suited for ML-based analysis.

  • Equipment condition monitoring
  • Produced-wafer quality prediction
  • Process-aware production scheduling

SMART-IC Opportunity

AI can learn patterns and relationships in the field data and turn them into decisions that improve cost-effectiveness and operational awareness.

The goal is not just analytics, but smarter monitoring, prediction, and production optimization.
