Part I: Introduction

The High Cost of Cleaning

Online Cleaning (Online-PTD)

Wiping probes on abrasive surfaces directly inside the testing machine.

  • Takes ~2 minutes
  • Disrupts immediate testing flow

Offline Cleaning (Offline-PTD)

Removing the probe card for detailed chemical and mechanical cleaning.

  • Takes 30+ minutes
  • Completely halts production line
Optimization Goal: Minimize unnecessary cleaning cycles while strictly preventing yield loss from pseudo-fails.
