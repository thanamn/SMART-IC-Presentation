The Wafer Probing Bottleneck

Electrical Wafer Sorting (EWS)

EWS is the crucial final testing phase before wafers are diced and packaged.

20,000+ Micro-probes per Card Touchdowns (TDs) Testing Contact

The Failure Mode

Over thousands of touchdowns, debris accumulates on microscopic probe tips.

Increases Contact Resistance (CRES), causing Pseudo-Fails, where perfectly good chips are falsely marked as defective.
