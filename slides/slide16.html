Part IV: Summary & Conclusion

An Ambitious Agenda, Open to DATE Feedback

Challenges to Address

The paper presents SMART-IC as an ambitious project whose full potential depends on solving important technical and operational challenges.

  • Understanding the limits of AI-guided factory optimization in real production settings.
  • Translating advanced models into robust, trusted decisions inside manufacturing workflows.
  • Identifying the practical constraints that matter most for deployment and scale-up.

Why DATE Interaction Matters

The DATE audience brings deep expertise in design, test, and manufacturing of electronic circuits and systems.

The goal is not only to present results, but to use community feedback to identify, understand, and tackle the project’s hardest challenges more effectively.
