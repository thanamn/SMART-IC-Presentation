Part I: Introduction

The SMART-IC Project Overview

Project Goal

To build a complete AI-enabled framework for smart monitoring and production optimization. Reducing defect generation and lowering waste at all levels of semiconductor manufacturing.

Timeline & Scope

Duration: Initial stage (2024) through end of 2025.

Budget: €300k Academic Project.
