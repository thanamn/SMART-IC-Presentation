Section I: Introduction

SMART-IC Project Overview

The paper presents SMART-IC as a complete framework built around two main pillars: an AI-powered engine and an enhanced MES.

Project Aim

Improve semiconductor manufacturing with smarter monitoring and production optimization, reducing defects and waste.

AI-Powered Engine

Extract useful knowledge from production and testing data for anomaly detection, forecasting, and synthetic data generation.

Enhanced MES

Use those AI outputs for production-aware scheduling, allocation, and smarter maintenance decisions.

Project Context

300k€ academic project funded by the Italian Ministry of University and Research and the Next Generation EU plan, with industrial support from STMicroelectronics and Technoprobe.
